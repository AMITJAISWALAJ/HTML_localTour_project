Ram Ghat Chitrakoot

Ram Ghat Chitrakoot →Where There is SHREE RAM in every partice.


Ram Ghat, which is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Uttar Pradesh, is believed to be the place where Lord Rama, Goddess Sita and Lord Laxman held an elaborate conversation with Saint Goswami Tulsidas. This is a major reason why it occupies a special place in the hearts and minds of the people.

In Ramghat lord Rama,Sita and Laxmana took a dip during their stay in chitrakoot . It is also believed that this is the place where lord rama and sita appeared to Tulsidas and composed ram charit manas to him .The place is holy and quite soothing and sacred and has a divine touch.

Many Places to visit in Ramghat

Sri Matya Gajendranath Mandir, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


The identity of the religious city Chitrakoot is associated with Lord Shriram. But here on the banks of Maa Mandakini, there is the world famous ancient Matyagjendra Nath Shiva temple at Ramghat. In which the description of the glory of Shivling established is also found in Shivpuran. It is believed that the Shivling established in this temple was established by Lord Brahma himself with his own hands. It is said that Lord Brahma performed a yagya at this place.

Ramghat Hanuman mandir, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


Huge Statue of Hanuman ji : And he is very beautiful.This is a nice ghat on the bank of Mandakini river. It's full of positive vibes and creates a very serene atmosphere. Many other things, attractions are around.

Maa Ganga Aarti, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


A must visit place in Chitrakoot especially in the evening around 6:30 pm to witness the Maa Mandakini Aarti. If you reach to Chitrakoot in the evening then Ram Ghat should be the first place to start with. Mesmarising view of Evening Aarti

Ramghat resturant, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


This is the best hotel available on ramghat and providing best services to tourism. services like foods , rooms etc.

Boating, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


Best place to spend your evening in chitrakoot. board ride for family is availabe for 120-150/- boats are very nice with LED lights rabbits and music system.they play bhajan to make your ride smoothing.

Murti shop, Ramghat, Chitrakoot


This is the shop availabe on ramghat where you can buy some unique and ancient things like murti,toys etc.
