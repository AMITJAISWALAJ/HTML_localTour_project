Welcome To Chitrakoot
'Hill of many Wonders'.

It falls in the Northern Vindhya Range of mountains spread over the state of Uttar Pradesh & Madhya Pradesh. Chitrakoot is one of the important places connected to the epic 'RAMAYANA'.


Your Dream Places To Visit In Chitrakoot


RAMGHAT

Ram Ghat, is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Uttar Pradesh, is believed to be the place where Lord Rama, Goddess Sita and Lord Laxman held an elaborate conversation with Saint Goswami Tulsidas.



GUPT-GODAVARI

Gupt Godavari is a famous cave in chitrakoot. It has two cave system inside a mountain with knee high water level. The bigger cave has two stone carved thrones believed to be belonging to Rama and Laxman.



HANUMAN-DHARA

Hanuman Dhara is one such place. Located about 3 km from Ramghat.This place is called Hanuman Dhara because of a gentle stream which flows over the statue of Lord Hanuman.



SHABARI-WATERFALL

In Shabri waterfall, 3 water bodies are falling down which is going further into a bigger water body that disappears in the forest. A complete view will hypnotize you with its unique beauty.



SATI-ANSUIYA-TEMPLE

Sati Anusuya Ashram is located in chitrakoot. It is one of the holiest places in the region and of the "chitrakoot char dham". This place gets its name from the fact that it served as the ashram of Maharishi Atri along with his wife Mahasati Anasuya.



Kamta Nath Temple

Kamta Nath Temple,Kamad giri is one of the holiest places of the Chitrakoot place.Kamtanath is one of the religious temples found in the Chitrakoot.



RAJAPUR

Rajapur is a town and tehsil of Chitrakoot district in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is situated along the bank of river Yamuna. It has its nickname as "Tulsi-Dhaam".



BHARAT-TEMPLE

The temple is known as the spot where Bharat met Lord Rama during his exile and persuaded him to return to Ayodhya and rule the kingdom. Legends says that the meeting of the two brothers was so overwhelming that it melted the rocks of Chitrakoot.



GANESH-BAGH

Ganesh Bagh is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Chitrakoot in Uttar Pradesh. Located on the Karvi-Devangana Road. The main attractions of this place is the temple, palace and the step well.



JANKI-KUND

It is located about 2km from Ramghat. There are two temples, Sankat Mochan Hanuman & Shree Raghuvir Temple. Across the kund lies the Pramod Van, whose presence plays a big role in enhancing the tranquillity of the place.



KALINJAR-FORT
